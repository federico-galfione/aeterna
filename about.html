About Us

Rooted in Italy,
Open to the World

We bridge the gap between international clients and Italy's extraordinary venues, sharing the places and traditions that make this country timeless.

Our Story

Born from a Simple Truth

Italy holds some of the world's most remarkable venues, places where history lives in every stone and celebrations feel eternal. Yet many of these venues remain hidden from international clients, lacking the infrastructure to reach beyond Italy's borders.

Aeterna Events was founded to close this gap. We combine deep local knowledge with international understanding, connecting clients worldwide with venues that would otherwise remain undiscovered. No contracted lists, no commissions that influence our recommendations, just honest guidance toward places that deserve to be known.

The name "Aeterna" is Latin for "eternal," reflecting both Italy's timeless heritage and our belief that the right venue creates memories that last beyond any single celebration.

Vision

What We See
